Online bookshop permanently closed

After 13 years of online trading and over 35,000 books sold, I have sadly come to the difficult decision to close the online shop as of January 2024.

I would like to thank all our lovely customers for supporting a small business over the years.

I will be bulk selling the remaining stock at local pop up events or other online market places. Keep an eye on Facebook or email me if you're interested in any books.

- Anne, Settle Down Books
